The American property index

TitleBlock stores and verifies properties by hand

My grandfather remortgaged some property he had owned for decades. The bank made him pay over $1,000 to verify the property was still his. All the documents existed they simply needed to be compiled and verified. Because things do change and if all these important documents live in one place where the proper parties could come and edit and verify these important property records then there wouldnt be a need to charge $1,000+ to a new home owner or a long time property owner to prove who owns what property. Property ownership matters to everyone. We verify records by hand and hold the parties that approve records accountable. No more paying hundreds and thousands of dollars for information already researched, compiled, and stored across a national network of computers and offices.
